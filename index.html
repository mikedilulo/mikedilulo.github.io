Gameplan

A team hub application for a coach to manage his players. This application allows you to have more than one team you are managing. The application allows you to create your team with your roster of players. You can also add notes to the board so your players are up to date with real time information, and also features up to date events(top 3). This application was built with a team of 4, with a Vue.js front-end, node.js back-end and MongoDB for the database.
